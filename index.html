Gameplay

Simon's Turn:
The Simon unit starts by flashing one colored button with a corresponding sound. As the game progresses, Simon adds more lights to the sequence, making it longer each time.

Your Turn:
Your job is to watch Simon's pattern and then press the colored buttons in the "exact same order".
If you repeat the sequence correctly, Simon will add another light, making the pattern longer.

Winning and Losing
If you make a mistake and press the wrong button you lose. Simon will show red color, and your score is displayed. The goal is to repeat as many sequences as possible, getting a higher score with each correct pattern.
